     ^101. Evam me sutaṃ. Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā Ānando

     ^102. Atha kho Subho māṇavo Todeyya-putto aññataraṃ
māṇavakaṃ#1 āmantesi: ‘Ehi tvaṃ māṇavaka, yena
samaṇo Ānando ten' upasaṃkama,#2 upasaṃkamitvā mama

{lahu-ṭṭhānaṃ} balaṃ phāsu-vihāraṃ puccha: “Subho māṇavo
Todeyya-putto bhavantaṃ Ānandaṃ appābādham appātaṅ-
kaṃ {lahu-ṭṭhānaṃ} balaṃ phāsu-vihāraṃ pucchatīti,” evañ
ca vadehi: “Sādhu kira bhavaṃ Ānando yena Subhassa
māṇavassa Todeyya-puttassa nivesanaṃ ten' upasaṃ-
kamatu anukampaṃ upādāyāti.”’
